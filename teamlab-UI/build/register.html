1. What happens once I place an order for an essay or paper?
You will set up a personal account by your email and a selected password. You may use this account to monitor the progress of your order. You may also upload additional instructions, samples of your own writing for your writer to review, and use the links to communicate with your writer or with our customer support team. Once you see all of the options on your account, you will see how easy it is to use.
